ABOUT THE FUNDS

We use your donations to help reduce carbon footprint and to make the world a better place. There are numerous things we can invest in from the funds to save the earth.

    01

    Growing plants

    From the funds that we get, we will use some of it to grow some plants that can help save our environment such as trees.

    They provide all animals oxygen which we all need to survive.

    Seeing plants is refreshing and connecting to the outdoors improves self-esteem, and overall mental health.

    02

    Reduce use of plastic

    The reason why we should reduce the use of plastic is because when plastic makes it to the landfills, they do not decompose like earth friendly materials. Also polluting our seas with plastic can harm seaflife.

    We could use some of the funds we get to help reduce the use of plastic bottles and bags.

    Making plastic bags cost money will make people think a second time about using plastic bags to possible help the Earth.

    03

    Implementing more solar panels

    Another way we could possibly use our funds for are to implement more solar panels for our energy source.

    Since we are using the energy from the sun, there will be less use of fossil fuel or generated electricity.

    Reducing the use of fossil fuel & generated electricity can reduce carbon footprint as burning the fuel can warm the Earth and produce greenhouse gases.

    04

    Investing in reusable products

    Investing in more reusable products will reduce the use of plastic bags and more plastic products.

    Even though people still might use a lot of plastic products, we could still raise awareness and convince some of our population to at least consider using more reusable products.

    If we invest in reusable products and let the world know about it, the world will overall be healthier and happier!
