About the Funds

  • Growing plant

    From the funds that we get, we will use some of it to grow some plants that can help save our environment such as trees. They provide a lot of animals oxygen which we all need to survive.

  • Reduce use of plastic

    We could use some of the funds we get to help reduce the use of plastic bottles and bags by making plastic bags cost money so that people will possibly think again if they should really get a plastic bag.

  • Implementing more solar panels

    Another way we could possibly use our funds for are to implement more solar panels for our energy source since we are using the energy from the sun as you can use less of fossil fuel or generated electricity.

  • Investing in reusable products

    Investing in more reusable products will reduce the use of plastic bags and more plastic products. Even though people still might use a lot of plastic products, we could still raise awareness and convince some of our population to at least consider using more reusable products.
